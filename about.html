About

Strategic advocacy, with integrity and confidentiality

A civil litigation practice built on careful preparation, clear advice, and steady representation before the courts of Patiala and the High Court.

Advocate Tejveer Singh Kharoud is a civil lawyer in Patiala, practising before the Hon'ble Punjab and Haryana High Court. His law chambers handle civil litigation, industrial dispute resolution, and recovery proceedings, acting for individuals, businesses and institutions across Patiala, Punjab, and the wider region.

As a litigation lawyer in Patiala, Advocate Tejveer Singh Kharoud specialises in contract enforcement, arbitration, and commercial disputes, matters that call for precise drafting, a firm grasp of procedure, and the patience to see a case through to resolution. He also advises as an industrial dispute lawyer on labour relations and statutory compliance, an area where prevention is often as valuable as litigation.

Recognised as a high court lawyer in Patiala, Advocate Tejveer Singh Kharoud carries matters beyond the district courts when escalation is the right course, providing the same strategic advocacy at every level while delivering legal representation with integrity, professionalism, and complete confidentiality throughout.
