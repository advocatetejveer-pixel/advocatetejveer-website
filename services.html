Services

Civil, commercial and industrial law services

Each matter is handled with full preparation, from the first consultation through to resolution. As a trusted Civil Litigation Lawyer Patiala, Advocate Tejveer Singh Kharoud provides strategic legal representation tailored to every client's needs. Below is an overview of the practice areas covered by this office.

01

Civil Litigation

Representation in civil suits and disputes before the District Courts, Patiala, from filing and pleadings through to trial and, where necessary, appeal.

  • Drafting and filing of civil suits
  • Trial representation and cross-examination
  • Injunctions and interim relief applications
  • Appeals and revisions arising from civil orders
02

Industrial Dispute Resolution

Advisory and representation in disputes between employers and employees, including matters before labour courts and industrial tribunals, alongside ongoing compliance guidance. Learn more about Advocate Tejveer Singh Kharoud's civil litigation practice and approach to resolving complex legal disputes.

  • Representation in industrial tribunal and labour court matters
  • Advisory on labour relations and workplace disputes
  • Statutory compliance guidance for employers
03

Recovery Proceedings

Suits and execution proceedings to recover money or property due, pursued through the appropriate civil process from demand through to enforcement.

  • Recovery of money suits
  • Execution and enforcement of decrees
  • Pre-litigation demand and settlement correspondence
04

Contract Enforcement

Drafting agreements that hold up under scrutiny, and acting where another party has failed to honour the terms they agreed to.

  • Drafting and review of commercial agreements
  • Breach of contract claims
  • Specific performance and damages claims
05

Arbitration

Drafting arbitration clauses at the contract stage, representing parties through arbitral proceedings, and enforcing or challenging awards before the courts.

  • Drafting and advising on arbitration clauses
  • Representation in arbitral proceedings
  • Enforcement and challenge of arbitral awards
06

Commercial Disputes

Representation in business-to-business disputes, partnership disagreements and other commercial matters requiring careful, confidential handling.

  • Partnership and business disputes
  • Commercial recovery and enforcement
  • Negotiated settlements where appropriate
07

High Court Advocacy

Representation before the Hon'ble Punjab and Haryana High Court, including writ petitions, appeals and revisions arising from lower court orders.

  • Writ petitions
  • Civil appeals and revisions
  • Interim relief applications before the High Court
