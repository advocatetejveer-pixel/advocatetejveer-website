Introduction

A civil litigation practice built on precision and discretion

Advocate Tejveer Singh Kharoud is a trusted Civil Lawyer Patiala practising before the Hon'ble Punjab and Haryana High Court. Based in Punjab, his chambers handle civil litigation, industrial dispute resolution and recovery proceedings, with a particular focus on contract enforcement, arbitration and commercial disputes.

As an industrial dispute lawyer, he also advises on labour relations, statutory compliance, and legal services that call for the same close attention to detail as any courtroom matter. Clients engage this office for strategic advocacy delivered with integrity and confidentiality at every stage of a dispute.
